An Abstraction of House Parties

29 June 2015

House parties are a common social gathering and pastime in the college ecosystem. There are many factors that attribute to the success rate of these events such as location, timing, gender ratio, available space, marketing, and music. This study hopes to examine the nature of them by accessing data from twitter and instagram in order to determine how successful it can be. We will be focusing on girl to guy ratio (emphasis on the girls) as well as time of day the events are posted to social networks. A friend of mine (wassup Cam!!) had a party on a pretty big weekend in our city and I figured that it would be the perfect time to collect some data.
