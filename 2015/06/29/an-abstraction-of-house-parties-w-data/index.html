An Abstraction of House Parties

29 June 2015

House parties are a common social gatherings and pastimes in the college ecosystems. There are many factors that attribute to the success rate of these events such as location, timing, gender ratio, available space, marketing, and music. This studies hopes to examine the nature of them by accessing data from twitter and instagram in order to determine how successful it can be. We will be focusing on girl to guy ratio (emphasis on the girls) as well as time of day the events are posted to social networks. A friend of mine (wassup Cam!!) had a party on a pretty big weekend in our city and I figured that it would be the perfect time to collect some data. Using google charts I was able to generate these cool charts. Check them out.
